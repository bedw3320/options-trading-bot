EM ETFs trade US hours but reference overnight markets. Chinese policy announcements, Brazilian central bank decisions, and currency devaluations create opening gaps that delta hedging cannot protect against.

This gap risk is a structural feature of EM ETF options — not a temporary condition. It's why [[EM short put delta should be 15-20 versus 20-25 for US equities due to gap risk]].
