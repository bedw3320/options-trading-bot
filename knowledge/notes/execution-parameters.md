# Execution Parameters

Quantitative parameters governing risk reversal trade construction and management.

## Position Sizing

- [[US equity risk reversal position size is 3-5 percent of portfolio notional]]
- [[EM ETF risk reversal position size is 2-3 percent reduced for higher volatility]]

## Strike and DTE

- [[optimal short put strike is 15-25 delta at 7-15 percent OTM where skew curve is richest]]
- [[EM short put delta should be 15-20 versus 20-25 for US equities due to gap risk]]
- [[30-45 DTE is the primary vehicle for 10 percent target moves]]
- [[60-90 DTE suitable for macro EM thesis plays targeting 20 percent moves]]

## Liquidity

- [[EM ETF options require monthly expirations only with minimum OI of 1000 contracts per leg]]
- [[SPY and US single names accept weekly options with minimum OI of 500 contracts]]
- [[bid-ask spread on each leg must be 10 percent or less of mid-market premium]]

## Roll and Exit

- [[at 21 DTE remaining assess position and roll to next monthly if thesis intact]]
- [[stop-loss at put strike breach plus 3 percent for US and 5 percent for EM]]
