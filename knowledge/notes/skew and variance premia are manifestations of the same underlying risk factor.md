The skew risk premium and the variance risk premium are manifestations of the same underlying risk factor. This has critical portfolio construction implications: combining a risk reversal strategy (harvesting skew premium) with a variance selling strategy (harvesting variance premium) does NOT provide the diversification benefit that combining independent alpha streams would suggest.

## Evidence

Kozhan, Neuberger & Schneider (2013), SSRN.
